Arsenal Football Club, founded in 1886, holds a storied legacy as one of English football's most iconic institutions. Manager Information

Mikel Arteta's tenure as manager of Arsenal Football Club has been characterized by a blend of promise, challenges, and gradual transformation. Appointed in December 2019, Arteta, a former Arsenal player and assistant coach under Pep Guardiola at Manchester City, brought a fresh perspective and tactical acumen to the Emirates Stadium. His emphasis on discipline, tactical organization, and player development quickly resonated with the squad, leading to significant improvements in performance and results. Arteta guided Arsenal to FA Cup glory in his debut season, showcasing his ability to inspire his team to success on the big stage. However, the road hasn't been without its hurdles, as the team navigates through periods of inconsistency and transition. Despite challenges, Arteta's vision for the club's future remains clear, emphasizing a commitment to attractive, possession-based football while instilling a winning mentality among his players. With time and support, Arteta aims to lead Arsenal back to the summit of English and European football, building a legacy that honors the club's illustrious history while forging a path toward future glory.
