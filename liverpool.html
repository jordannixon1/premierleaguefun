Team Overview

Liverpool Football Club is a storied institution in the world of English football, boasting a rich history and passionate fanbase. Founded in 1892, the club is based in Liverpool, England, and competes in the Premier League, the highest tier of English football. Known affectionately as "The Reds," Liverpool has garnered immense success both domestically and internationally.
